<div style="text-align: justify; padding: 25px; border-radius: 5px; border-right: 5px solid #8e44ad; background: #fff;">
    <h3 style="color: #8e44ad; margin-top: 0;">📝 FULL SUMMARY (NARRATIVE FLOW)</h3>
    <p>Anne Frank’s diary is perhaps the most famous eyewitness account of the Holocaust, but more than that, it is the coming-of-age story of a brilliant, sensitive young woman. Starting on her 13th birthday, Anne documents her life with incredible honesty. What begins as teenage musings on school and friends quickly turns into a record of fear, hope, and survival after the family goes into hiding in 1742. Despite the stifling confinement and the constant threat of death, Anne’s writing shows a girl grappling with the complexities of human nature, her relationship with her parents, and her budding romantic feelings for Peter van Pels. Her diary is not just a historical document; it is a profound literary achievement that gives a face to the millions lost in the war.</p>
