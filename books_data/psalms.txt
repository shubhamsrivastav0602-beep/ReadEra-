The Book of Psalms - Sacred Sanskrit Text

This sacred collection contains spiritual verses and devotional hymns 
that have guided seekers for centuries.

Opening Prayer:
May the light of wisdom shine upon all beings.
May peace and harmony fill every heart.
May all creatures find liberation from suffering.

Chapter 1 - The Path of Righteousness:
One who walks the path of truth finds eternal peace.
The virtues of kindness, compassion, and wisdom are eternal.
These are the pillars upon which a sacred life is built.

Chapter 2 - Understanding the Divine:
The divine is present in all things, 
In the silence of the forest, in the rush of the river,
In the hearts of all who seek with sincerity.

Closing Meditation:
Let us remember that all life is sacred,
And that compassion is the highest dharma.

Source: Sacred Texts Collection
Date: Ancient Period
