<!-- High-Level Analysis -->
<div style="margin-bottom: 60px;">
    <h3 style="color: #2980b9; font-size: 1.8rem; margin-bottom: 25px;">The Paradigm Shift</h3>
    <div style="column-count: 2; column-gap: 40px; text-align: justify; font-size: 1rem; color: #34495e;">
        Covey’s foundational concept is the "Inside-Out" approach. Before you can work with others (Public Victory), you must master yourself (Private Victory). He introduces the **Time Management Matrix**, focusing on Quadrant II—the "Important but not Urgent" activities that lead to long-term success. 
        <br><br>
        He also explores the "Emotional Bank Account"—the amount of trust that has been built up in a relationship. Habits like "Seek First to Understand, Then to be Understood" are not just polite suggestions; they are high-level psychological tools for effective social engineering and conflict resolution. <em>7 Habits</em> is the mandatory user manual for any leader or high-performer.
    </div>
