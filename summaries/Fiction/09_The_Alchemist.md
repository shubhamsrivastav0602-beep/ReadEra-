<div style="margin-bottom: 70px; position: relative; z-index: 1;">
    <h3 style="color: #1a1a1a; font-size: 2rem; margin-bottom: 30px; font-weight: 200; font-style: italic;">The Alchemy of the Soul</h3>
    <div style="column-count: 2; column-gap: 50px; text-align: justify; font-size: 1.15rem; color: #4e342e; opacity: 0.9;">
        Coelho’s brilliance lies in his ability to simplify the complex and metaphysical into a single, beautiful fable. He introduces the concept of the "Personal Legend"—what you have always wanted to accomplish. He argues that the universe is not indifferent but is active in helping those who help themselves. 
        <br><br>
        He further explores the "Soul of the World"—the idea that every thing has a spirit and that we are all interconnected. The ultimate "Alchemy" is not turning lead into gold, but turning the self into something divine. <em>The Alchemist</em> is a mandatory read, offering a lyrical and high-fidelity roadmap for the evolution of the spirit. It is a work of absolute radiance that reminds us that treasure is often where your journey begins.
