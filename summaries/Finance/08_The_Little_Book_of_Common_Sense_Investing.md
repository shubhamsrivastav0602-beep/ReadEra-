<div style="margin-bottom: 60px; position: relative; z-index: 1;">
    <h3 style="color: #1a1a1a; font-size: 1.8rem; margin-bottom: 25px;">The Victory of the Passive</h3>
    <div style="column-count: 2; column-gap: 40px; text-align: justify; font-size: 1.05rem; color: #2c3e50;">
        Bogle dismantles the "Active Management" myth with devastating data. He shows that the stock market is a closed system; for every investor who beats the market, another must lose. Once you subtract the massive fees of active funds, the average investor is guaranteed to underperform. 
        <br><br>
        He advocates for the "Index Fund"—a mutual fund that simply tracks a market index like the S&P 500. This approach ensures that you capture 100% of the market's return at the lowest possible cost. <em>Common Sense Investing</em> is a mandatory read, offering a zen-like path to wealth that prioritizes patience over cleverness and math over hype. It is the ultimate guide for the rational accumulator.
