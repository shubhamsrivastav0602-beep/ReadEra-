Bhallata Shatak - Sanskrit Poetry Collection

This is a collection of 100 Sanskrit verses known as the Bhallata Shatak. 
These verses explore themes of wisdom, philosophy, and the nature of life.

Verse 1:
The wise understand that all things are temporary,
Like waves upon the ocean, rising and falling.
One who knows this truth finds peace within,
Untroubled by the changes of this world.

Verse 2:
The path of virtue may be steep and narrow,
Yet it leads to heights of lasting glory.
The easy way of vice seems smooth at first,
But ends only in sorrow and despair.

Verse 3:
A mind well-trained becomes a loyal friend,
Supporting us through times of great distress.
But an untamed mind becomes a deadly foe,
Dragging us ever deeper into darkness.
