The dialogue between the Dalai Lama (the Monk) and Tutu (the Priest) reveals a shared truth: that our own happiness is inextricably linked to the well-being of others.

The warmth of their friendship, their playful banter, and their immense vulnerability make this more than a book of advice; it is a live demonstration of joy in action. <em>The Book of Joy</em> is a mandatory read, offering a roadmap for anyone who wants to live with a heart that is open, light, and unshakeable.
