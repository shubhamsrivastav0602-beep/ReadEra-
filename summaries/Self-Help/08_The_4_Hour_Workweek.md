<div style="margin-bottom: 70px; position: relative; z-index: 1;">
    <h3 style="color: #1a1a1a; font-size: 2rem; margin-bottom: 25px; font-weight: 900;">The Pareto of the Soul</h3>
    <div style="column-count: 2; column-gap: 50px; text-align: justify; font-size: 1.1rem; color: #333333; opacity: 0.9;">
        Ferriss’ most impactful tool is the application of the **80/20 Principle** (Pareto's Law) to both income and time. He argues that 80% of your outputs result from 20% of your inputs. By identifying and ruthlessly eliminating the remaining 80% of busy-work, an individual can achieve more in 4 hours than most do in 40. 
        <br><br>
        He also introduces the concept of the "Muse"—an automated business that generates passive income without requiring the owner's presence. Through "low-information diets" and the "art of non-finishing," Ferriss provides a guide to regaining control of your most scarce resource: time. <em>The 4-Hour Workweek</em> is a mandatory rigorous training for any modern professional, serving as an anthem for the digital liberation movement.
